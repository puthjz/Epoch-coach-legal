## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get started?

After downloading Epoch Coach, create an account and add your income and expenses. The AI will use this information to provide personalized financial guidance tailored to your situation.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel or change your plan:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Epoch Coach**
5. Choose **Cancel Subscription** or change your plan

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

### What subscription plans are available?

- **Monthly Premium:** 349 THB/month
- **Annual Premium:** 2,990 THB/year (save 29%)

Both plans include full AI financial coaching, goal tracking, spending insights, financial education, and an ad-free experience.

### Is my financial data secure?

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell or share your personal financial data with third parties. For full details, please read our [Privacy Policy](./index.md).

### The app is crashing or not loading properly

Please try the following:

1. Make sure you are running the latest version of Epoch Coach
2. Restart the app
3. Restart your device
4. Check that your iOS is up to date
5. Delete and reinstall the app (your account data will be preserved)

If the issue persists, contact us at epochcoach.info@gmail.com with a description of the problem, your device model, and iOS version.

### How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app under **Settings > Account > Delete Account**. This will permanently remove all your data. This action cannot be undone.

### I was charged but don't have premium access

This can happen if there is a delay in processing. Please try:

1. Restart the app
2. Go to **Settings > Restore Purchases**

If the issue continues, contact us with your Apple ID email and approximate date of purchase.
