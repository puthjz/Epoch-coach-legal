## 2. Definitions

* **"Personal Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under the PDPA.
* **"Processing"** means any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, storage, use, disclosure, combination, erasure, or destruction.
* **"Data Processor"** means a third-party entity that processes personal data on our behalf and under our instructions.
* **"Sensitive Data"** means personal data relating to racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, criminal records, genetic data, biometric data, health data, or any data that may affect the data subject in a similar manner. Financial information, while sensitive in everyday usage, is not classified as "Sensitive Data" under the PDPA.

## 6. AI Data Processing

Epoch Coach uses AI-powered features to generate personalized coaching content. These features send data to OpenAI's API. We are transparent about exactly what is sent, why, and how your consent is obtained.

### 6.1 AI Coach Chat (Coach Tab)

The AI Coach Chat allows you to ask financial questions and receive responses grounded in your actual current situation. When you send a message, the following data is transmitted to OpenAI's API:

* The text of your message
* Your current financial cycle window (start and end dates)
* Your income and spending totals for the current cycle
* Your top spending categories for the current cycle
* Your chosen financial path (Easy, Balanced, or Hard) and the corresponding savings rate
* Your current daily spending guide
* Your savings goals (name, target amount, current amount)
* Your debts (name, remaining balance, minimum payment)
* Whether you are currently in Stability Focus mode

This contextual data is sent so that the AI's response is grounded in your actual financial situation rather than generic advice. Without this context, the AI's responses would be inaccurate or unhelpful.

**No personally identifying information is included in the request.** Your name, email address, account ID, and contact details are never sent to OpenAI as part of Coach Chat queries.

> **Consent:** Before your first message is sent, the app displays a clear consent notice explaining what data will be transmitted to OpenAI. You must tap "I Agree" to proceed. If you decline, no data is sent. This consent is stored on your device and only requested once.

### 6.2 Daily Coach Messages

The Service generates short daily coaching messages that appear as notifications and on the home screen (such as morning greetings, evening check-ins, and stability-focus acknowledgments). These messages are produced by OpenAI based on a curated set of message templates and your current language preference. No personal financial data is sent to OpenAI for these daily messages — only your language preference and a request to generate an appropriately-toned message.

### 6.3 Third-Party AI Recipient: OpenAI

Both AI features send data to OpenAI (OpenAI, LLC, United States). OpenAI processes this data on our behalf as a Data Processor under a data processing agreement. According to OpenAI's API data usage policy, data submitted through the API is not used to train or improve OpenAI's models by default.

OpenAI's Privacy Policy is available at: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

### 6.4 Data Minimization

We apply data minimization principles to all AI processing. The Coach Chat sends only the financial context necessary to ground a response in your current situation — no historical data, no personal identifiers, no metadata about your account. Daily message generation sends no personal data at all.

### 6.5 AI Output Storage

Daily coach messages generated for you are stored on your account in our Convex database and associated with your user ID. AI Coach Chat responses are displayed in the app but are not stored on our servers — they exist only in your local conversation view during the session.

> **Important:** AI-generated content is not financial advice. The AI may produce inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate responses. Always exercise your own judgment regarding financial decisions.

Free users of Epoch Coach see advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC, United States). Premium subscribers do not see ads.

### 8.1 Data Collected by Google AdMob

To serve advertisements, Google AdMob may collect and use the following data from your device:

* General device information (device type, OS version)
* Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, ad view duration)

### 8.2 App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — iOS

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, Epoch Coach will display a system-level permission dialog shortly after you open the app for the first time. This dialog clearly explains that the app would like to track your activity for personalized advertising.

* **If you tap Allow:** AdMob may use your IDFA to serve personalized ads.
* **If you tap "Ask App Not to Track":** AdMob will serve non-personalized ads only. Your IDFA is not accessed or shared.

You can change this permission at any time in: **Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Epoch Coach**.

### 8.3 Third-Party Ad Network: Google AdMob

Advertisements are delivered by Google AdMob (Google LLC, United States). Google's data collection and use for advertising is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 8.4 What We Do NOT Share With Advertisers

We do not share your financial data, transaction history, income, goals, debts, AI interactions, or any personal financial information with Google AdMob or any advertising partner. Advertisers receive only anonymized ad performance data (impressions and clicks) through the AdMob platform.

## 9. Data Storage & Security

### 9.1 Storage Infrastructure

Server-side data is stored in Convex, a cloud database platform. Authentication data is managed by Clerk. Both services employ industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

### 9.2 On-Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

* Language preference (EN/TH) — stored in AsyncStorage; not synced to any server
* AI Coach consent flag — stored in AsyncStorage on-device only; not synced to any server
* Authentication session tokens — stored in Expo Secure Store (encrypted, hardware-backed where available); cleared on logout

When you start entering a transaction in the log card and then background the app without saving, your in-progress entry is preserved in the app's working memory only. It is not written to disk or synced to any server. If you fully close the app, the entry is discarded.

### 9.3 Security Measures

* All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
* Authentication is managed via Clerk with short-lived JWT session tokens
* On-device tokens are stored in hardware-backed secure storage (Expo Secure Store)
* Server-side access is authenticated and authorized on every API request
* We do not store passwords — authentication is fully delegated to Clerk
* Webhook communications are secured via SVIX with signature verification

### 9.4 Security Limitations

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
