## 4. Description of the Service

Epoch Coach is a personal finance companion application that provides:

* Manual transaction logging via the home screen log card (expenses and income with category, amount, and optional debt or goal allocation)
* Goal-directed daily pacing that adjusts as you log transactions and progress through your financial cycle
* Stability Focus mode, which automatically adjusts your daily guidance during challenging cycles
* Financial cycle management with a configurable cycle anchor day
* Savings goal and debt management tools
* AI-powered coaching via the Coach tab, with conversational responses grounded in your actual financial situation
* Daily notification companion system with four configurable tiers (Off / Less / Medium / Full)
* Financial health scoring and behavioral insights
* Wish list management
